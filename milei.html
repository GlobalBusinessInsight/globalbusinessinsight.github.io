阿根廷特别报告 Special Report: Argentina

电锯与奇迹 The Chainsaw and the Miracle

哈维尔·米莱执政两周年：从恶性通胀边缘到财政盈余，一场激进的自由主义实验如何重塑阿根廷。 Javier Milei's two years in power: From the brink of hyperinflation to fiscal surplus, how a radical libertarian experiment is reshaping Argentina.

一、 “电锯计划”：休克疗法与政策风暴 I. The "Chainsaw Plan": Shock Therapy & Policy Storm

米莱的竞选象征“电锯”，成为其削减公共支出的生动隐喻。 Milei's campaign symbol, the chainsaw, became a vivid metaphor for slashing public spending.

当 哈维尔·米莱（Javier Milei）于2023年12月入主玫瑰宫时，他继承的是一个满目疮痍的经济体：通胀率奔向200%，央行储备净值为负，财政赤字失控。他的回应是阿根廷历史上最激进的“休克疗法”。 W hen Javier Milei entered the Casa Rosada in December 2023, he inherited an economy in ruins: inflation sprinting towards 200%, net central bank reserves in the red, and a fiscal deficit spiraling out of control. His response was the most radical "shock therapy" in Argentine history.

核心政策清单 (2023-2025) Key Policy Inventory (2023-2025)

  • 货币贬值与汇率并轨： 上任首周将比索官方汇率贬值54%（从366贬至800），并实行每月2%的爬行钉住汇率制（Crawling Peg），大幅缩小了黑市汇率差。 Devaluation & Convergence: Devalued the peso by 54% in week one (366 to 800), implementing a 2% monthly crawling peg, sharply narrowing the gap with the black market rate.
  • “零赤字”财政铁律： 砍掉了一半的政府部门（从18个减至9个），停止公共工程招标，削减对各省的转移支付，并大幅削减能源和交通补贴。 "Zero Deficit" Iron Rule: Slashed ministries by half (18 to 9), halted public works tenders, cut transfers to provinces, and drastically reduced energy and transport subsidies.
  • 综合法案 (Omnibus Law)： 尽管初期受阻，最终版本的法案通过了国会，授予总统在行政、经济、金融和能源领域的紧急权力，并推动了国有企业的私有化进程。 The Omnibus Law: Despite initial hurdles, a modified version passed Congress, granting emergency powers in administrative, economic, financial, and energy sectors, and advancing privatization.
  • 解除管制 (DNU)： 废除了租赁法、供应法等数百项限制商业自由的法规，试图将阿根廷经济从高度管制的“社团主义”转向自由市场。 Deregulation (DNU): Repealed hundreds of regulations like the Rental Law and Supply Law, aiming to shift Argentina from regulated corporatism to a free market.

二、 宏观数据：痛苦后的复苏迹象 II. Macro Data: Signs of Recovery After Pain

米莱的政策在2024年造成了深度的经济衰退，但也成功以此为代价遏制了恶性通胀。进入2025年，阿根廷经济数据开始呈现出一种“V型”反转的态势。 Milei's policies induced a deep recession in 2024, a price paid to avert hyperinflation. Entering 2025, data began to show signs of a "V-shaped" reversal.

从2023年12月的25.5%峰值，降至2025年下半年的2%区间。 From a 25.5% peak in Dec 2023 to the 2% range in late 2025.

财政奇迹 (Fiscal Surplus) Fiscal Miracle

2024年第一季度，阿根廷实现了自2008年以来的首次季度财政盈余。这一趋势在2025年得以延续，证明了“电锯”削减支出的有效性，尽管其代价是公共服务的缩减。 In Q1 2024, Argentina achieved its first quarterly fiscal surplus since 2008. This trend continued into 2025, proving the efficacy of the "chainsaw," albeit at the cost of reduced public services.

GDP增长 (GDP Growth) GDP Growth

经历2024年约1.7%的萎缩后，IMF和阿根廷央行预测2025年GDP将反弹增长约4.5%。农业出口的恢复和能源部门（Vaca Muerta页岩油气）的繁荣是主要动力。 After contracting ~1.7% in 2024, the IMF and Central Bank project a rebound of ~4.5% in 2025. Agricultural recovery and the energy boom (Vaca Muerta) are key drivers.

三、 社会现实：贫困、抗议与忍耐 III. Social Reality: Poverty, Protest & Patience

2024年初的总罢工：工会走上街头高呼“祖国不容变卖”，抗议米莱的紧缩政策。（图源：半岛电视台） The General Strike of early 2024: Unions took to the streets chanting "The country is not for sale," protesting Milei's austerity.

宏观数据的改善并未立即转化为微观层面的幸福。米莱的改革是一场残酷的社会实验，阿根廷中下层阶级承担了最大的调整成本。 Macro improvements did not immediately translate to micro-level well-being. Milei's reform is a brutal social experiment, with the lower and middle classes bearing the brunt of the adjustment costs.

贫困率的过山车 (Poverty Rollercoaster) Poverty Rollercoaster

2024年初的货币大幅贬值导致物价飞涨，实际工资暴跌，贫困率一度飙升至55%（阿根廷天主教大学UCA数据）。然而，随着2025年通胀回落和工资逐步追赶，贫困率回落至32%左右，回到了这一轮危机前的水平。 Devaluation in early 2024 caused prices to soar and real wages to crash, pushing poverty to 55% (UCA data). However, as inflation cooled and wages caught up in 2025, poverty receded to ~32%, returning to pre-crisis levels.

阿根廷贫困率变化 (2023-2025) Argentina Poverty Rate Trends (2023-2025)

社会抗议的常态化与疲劳： 2024年1月，阿根廷爆发了针对新政府的首次全国性大罢工。然而，随着时间推移，虽然抗议从未停止（尤其是大学经费削减引发的学生抗议），但公众似乎对“必要的痛苦”展现出了惊人的忍耐力。民调显示，尽管生活艰难，米莱的核心支持率依然保持在45%-50%的稳固区间，这源于民众对旧有的庇隆主义政治精英的彻底失望。 Protest Normalization & Fatigue: A general strike erupted in Jan 2024. While protests persisted (notably students against budget cuts), the public showed surprising resilience to "necessary pain." Polls show Milei's core support remains solid at 45-50%, fueled by total disillusionment with the old Peronist elite.
