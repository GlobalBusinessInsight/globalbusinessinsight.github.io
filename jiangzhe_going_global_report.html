The New Silk Road: Jiangzhe's Manufacturing Vanguard

Business & Tech • March 15, 2026 • Hangzhou, China

As globalization shifts, enterprises rooted in the Yangtze River Delta are aggressively expanding their manufacturing and supply chain footprints across North America, Europe, and Latin America.

By MARK PIERRE & GBI RESEARCH

A comprehensive analysis of top-tier going-global enterprises headquartered in Zhejiang, focusing on overseas revenues exceeding $100M.

The industrial heartland of China, specifically Hangzhou and the broader Zhejiang province, is no longer just the "factory of the world." It is becoming the headquarters of global multinational corporations. Faced with shifting tariffs and the need for supply chain resilience, these manufacturing heavyweights are deploying capital to build factories in Mexico, Eastern Europe, and the United States. This report details five critical enterprises that fit the criteria: deep roots in Jiangzhe, heavy manufacturing/tech focus, over $100M in overseas revenue, and a localized footprint in target continents.
