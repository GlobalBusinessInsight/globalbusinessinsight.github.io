Step 1: Automated Invoice Ingestion

The process begins by eliminating manual handoffs. Invoices are automatically fetched from designated corporate email inboxes, ensuring they enter the processing queue instantly and without manual intervention. This initial automation step prevents delays and eliminates the labor of sorting and distributing incoming documents.

Step 2: Centralized Creation & SAP Synchronization

Within the platform, an accountant creates the invoice on a single, intuitive screen. The system uses intelligent data capture to pre-fill information and performs a real-time, two-way synchronization with SAP to validate and enrich data against master records (PO, Vendor, G/L). Once validated, the invoice is automatically posted to SAP, ensuring data integrity and prompt liability recording.

Step 3: Intelligent and Flexible Approval Routing

Digitized invoices are routed through pre-defined, configurable approval workflows. Approvers can review and action requests from anywhere via the EO platform, email, or integrated OA systems. This accelerates approval cycles, ensures adherence to corporate policies, and maintains a complete, auditable trail of all actions.

Step 4: Versatile Payment Execution

Upon final approval, the system facilitates secure payment execution across multiple methods. It can generate files for ACH, EFT, and Wire transfers, support customizable cheque printing, or process payments via credit card. This flexibility meets diverse supplier preferences and internal treasury policies efficiently.

Step 5: Automated Reconciliation and Clearing

The final loop is closed with automation. The system automatically fetches bank statements and uses intelligent matching with smart recommendations to reconcile payments. Once reconciled, it automatically triggers the clearing of the corresponding open items in SAP, ensuring the A/P sub-ledger is always accurate and up-to-date.

A Deep Dive into Key Capabilities

Essential Operation is more than a workflow; it's a rich platform of features designed to provide complete control and compliance over your financial operations.

Robust Vendor Master Data Management

Ensure a single source of truth with real-time synchronization of vendor master data with SAP. Supplement core data with payment-critical information like bank details and preferred payment methods. This minimizes errors, streamlines payments, and improves supplier relations.

Automated VAT & Withholding Tax

Navigate complex tax scenarios with automated VAT and Withholding Tax (WHT) calculations based on configurable rules. The system handles tiered rates and integrates seamlessly with SAP for correct G/L posting, ensuring accuracy and compliance with local regulations like Mexico's ISR.

Seamless Proforma & Prepayment Workflows

Formalize the management of advance payments. Create proforma invoices, manage prepayment approvals, and automatically post to SAP upon reconciliation. The "Actualize" function converts the proforma to a formal invoice once goods are received, ensuring proper expense recognition.

Integrated Tax Authority Certification

Ensure invoice authenticity with direct API integration to tax authority systems. As exemplified by Mexico's SAT CFDI validation, the system automatically verifies electronic invoices in real-time, preventing fraud and guaranteeing compliance in regulated markets.
