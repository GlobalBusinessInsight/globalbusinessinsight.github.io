ProcessGo Plus | Essentials - In Detail

Global HR and Payroll

Versatile payroll management with country-specific schemas, automated EFT/ACH, and "Tap to Pay" for direct deposits. Application allows input of work hours, payable items, and offers flexibility for payroll calculations. Manages and sends payroll statements efficiently.

Expense Management (Mobile App)

Seamlessly manage employee expense requests. Submit claims with details (type, amount, date) and attach receipts via the app. Automated approval requests, integration with accounting systems for posting rule mappings, and real-time status tracking of submissions.

Receivable and Payable Automation

Capture and integrate billing/invoices with SAP, archiving with PDF/XML. Fetch bank statements and reconcile within Essential, posting back to SAP. Supports tax authority integration and country compliance (VAT deductible, DIOT, T2).

ProcessGo Plus | Technology - In Detail

BPMN Integration

Innovate with BPMN to streamline, visualize, and transform processes. Design, analyze, and communicate processes effectively with user-friendly tools. Build once and reuse customized Scripts and Connectors within the ProcessGo Plus System Integration Tool.

ProcessGo Plus | Supply Chain - In Detail

OMNI Exchange Gateway

Get real-time information across systems and create interactive visualizations. Gain insights into sales, inventory, and financial performance to manage current business and forecast future performance. Analyze overall performance, specific channels/SKUs, and get instant alerts for out-of-stocks.

Warehousing Gateway

Define multi-storage structure with warehouse area design, displaying storage locations and bin utilization. Visualize active storage areas, track bins, and access key details. Add/configure new storage zones. Color-coded status indicators, QR code access to shipment records, label printing preferences, and ERP communication.
